Habit Tracker Mobile App

A habit tracker mobile app is a highly valuable tool for helping users establish and maintain positive health-related habits in their daily lives. Throughout the entire design and development process, it's essential to keep the user at the center of the design decisions, ensuring that the app meets their needs effectively and provides a seamless, engaging, intuitive, and enjoyable experience for habit formation and tracking.

This app was designed to simplify the process of habit tracking while making it highly engaging and visually appealing. It focuses on motivating users to build sustainable routines through easy-to-use features, clear navigation, and detailed real-time progress tracking. With this project, I aimed to create a solution that not only supports users in forming positive habits but also enhances their overall journey toward better consistent self-improvement and long-term personal productivity.

About the project

This habit tracker mobile app was developed as part of my internship during my graduation. The project aimed to help users create, manage, and track habits effectively while offering a visually appealing and user-friendly interface.

My Role in this project:-
  • During my three-month internship, I was responsible for developing this app using React Native technology.
  • My team leader provided the app's Figma designs, and I converted these designs into a fully functional application.
  • Worked on implementing responsive designs, user-friendly navigation, and interactive features to ensure an engaging user experience.
  • Gained hands-on experience in React Native development, strengthening my knowledge of cross-platform mobile app creation.
Learning Outcomes:-
  • Built expertise in React Native technology by converting Figma designs into an operational app.
  • Improved my understanding of state management, UI/UX principles, and performance optimization in mobile app development.
  • Enhanced teamwork and communication skills by collaborating effectively with team members.
  • Strengthened my ability to create visually appealing and high-performing mobile applications.

This project not only deepened my technical knowledge but also prepared me for tackling real-world challenges in mobile app development.
